INFORMATION

Over View of RST School-Bangladesh


RST School Bangladesh is a government approved private university founded in 2001 by RST company. The university is an independent organization with its own Board of Trustees.

Vision


RST SCHOOL BANGLADESH envisions promoting professionals and excellent leadership catering to the technological progress and development needs of the country.

Mission


RST SCHOOL BANGLADESH is committed to provide quality and excellent computer-based academic programs responsive to the emerging challenges of the time. It is dedicated to nurture and produce competent world class professional imbued with strong sense of ethical values ready to face the competitive world of arts, business, science, social science and technology.

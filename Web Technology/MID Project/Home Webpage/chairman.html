RST has always been a family. Spearheaded by my older brothers, MS. KST Khan, the Founder Member, Treasurer, and Vice President of HR & Administration, and Mr. XST Khan, the Founder Member, Former Chairman, and the Vice President of PR & International Affairs, myself as the Founder Member and Vice President of Student Affairs, together with our honorable Vice Chancellor, Mr. STR Khan, we have all seen RST School come a long way since its inception.
